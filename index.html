Baahubali is an Indian epic action media franchise created by director S. S. Rajamouli. Set in the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati, it follows the power struggle between two cousins—Amarendra Baahubali and Bhallaladeva—for the throne. The story combines themes of loyalty, betrayal, justice, and sacrifice. Known for its massive scale, visual grandeur, and emotionally rich storytelling, the franchise revolutionized Indian cinema, leading to the rise of the “pan-Indian film” movement. The two films, *Baahubali: The Beginning* (2015) and *Baahubali 2: The Conclusion* (2017), became global hits and the highest-grossing Indian films of their time. The franchise expanded into novels, comics, and animated series.

Maps

Google Maps is a web-based mapping and navigation service developed by Google. It allows users to view maps, explore locations, get driving, walking, cycling, and public transport directions, and track real-time traffic updates. Available on both web and mobile apps, it provides features like Street View, satellite imagery, terrain view, and indoor maps for locations like malls and airports. Users can search for places such as restaurants, hotels, or ATMs and view reviews, photos, and ratings. Google Maps also supports offline maps and voice-guided navigation. It has become one of the most widely used tools for travel, navigation, and local business discovery.
